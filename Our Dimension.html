Alphea

Dimension of Magic

Alphea Town

Alphea is the dimension of magic, it's a big world ruled by the lineage of the Sun and the Moon. Alphea was created by the Sun and the Moon to protect their people since they were attacked by humans thousands years ago. There is a castle where the Royal family lives and other people who have a high rank in the society.

Community

Near of the castle, is the town. Creatures mythology lives in Alphea as well as centaurus, gnomes, minotaur, sirens, etc. Of course, witches and warlocks lives here, there are two types of magicians; one can do magic by the four elements of nature (air, water, earth and fire) and the others are half human and half animal. After the war, Alpheans lives in harmony, they always help each other, they are smiling all the time and they pretty enjoy to do a lot of parties. The community are dancing and singing together everywhere, no matter what are you.
